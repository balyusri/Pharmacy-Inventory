Welcome

Located in MMU, Melaka, Pharmacy Inventory System is an affiliate of Novartis which provides innovative healthcare solutions that address the evolving needs of patients and societies. Headquartered in Basel, Switzerland, Novartis offers a diversified portfolio to best meet these needs: innovative medicines, cost-saving generic and biosimilar pharmaceuticals and eye care. Novartis Group companies employ approximately 118,000 full-time-equivalent associates and its products are sold in approximately 155 countries around the world.
